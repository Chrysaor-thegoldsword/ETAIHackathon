# Impact Model: ET Money Mentor

This document quantifies the potential business impact of the ET Money Mentor proof‑of‑concept.  The numbers below are illustrative and should be refined with real user data.

## Assumptions

- **User base**: 100,000 Economic Times readers per month visit finance and wealth content.
- **Adoption rate**: 8 % of visitors start the Money Mentor conversation, and 50 % of those complete the onboarding (providing income, expenses and goals).
- **Conversion rate**: 5 % of completed users purchase a recommended product (mutual fund or insurance) via ET’s affiliate partners.
- **Commission per sale**: ₹1,000 on average.
- **Cost of human financial advisor**: ₹25,000 per year for basic advice【47679722380880†L330-L352】.  It is assumed that half of Money Mentor users would have considered a human advisor and can now avoid or defer that cost.
- **User time saved**: The do‑it‑yourself (DIY) onboarding takes 10 minutes compared to roughly 5 hours of independent research (reading articles, comparing regimes, calculating SIPs, etc.).

## Calculations

### Revenue generated

- Potential paying users per month = 100,000 × 0.08 × 0.5 × 0.05 = **200** users.
- Monthly revenue from commissions = 200 users × ₹1,000 = **₹2 Lakh**.
- Annualized, this yields **₹24 Lakh** in commission revenue.

### Cost savings for users

- Users avoiding a human advisor = 100,000 × 0.08 × 0.5 × 0.5 = **2,000** users per month (assuming 50 % would have engaged a planner).
- Annual advisory fees avoided = 2,000 × ₹25,000 = **₹5 Crore**.
- Since the app is free, these savings translate into goodwill and increased engagement for ET.  Even if only 10 % of the avoided cost is captured via premium subscriptions or partner deals, that’s **₹50 Lakh** in annual value.

### Time saved

- Each user saves approximately 4 hours and 50 minutes compared with self‑research.  For 5,000 users per month (the 8 % who complete onboarding), that’s **24,000 hours saved monthly** – the equivalent of **12 full‑time analyst years** per year.

## Summary

ET Money Mentor delivers value on three fronts:

1. **Revenue upside** through affiliate commissions on recommended products (₹24 Lakh/year under conservative assumptions).
2. **User savings** by eliminating or postponing the need for a paid advisor (up to ₹50 Lakh/year captured via other monetization).
3. **Time savings** – a massive reduction in the time spent navigating personal finance, which translates into increased trust and engagement with the Economic Times ecosystem.

These back‑of‑the‑envelope numbers underscore why a personalized, agentic finance planner is a compelling opportunity for ET.
